You can tackle these in any order you wish. I recommend expanding the Fundamentals and Toolbox sections so that you can see what’s there. Come back to them if you encounter things you don’t understand in the other videos.

Are you impatient? If so, go straight for the 'Layers Tutorial' section and consult the other sections for things you don’t understand.

Do you like to be organised? If so, go through the ‘All about Layers’ section until you want to see them in action, at which point go through the 'Layers Tutorial' section, and finish the rest off later.
