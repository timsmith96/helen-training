About me

Hello and welcome to my website. My name is Helen Bonnor and I teach classes in Photoshop, Lightroom, photography, and other software, usually in person – for Eastleigh College, at the Winchester Discovery Centre and for the Winchester Photographic Society (WPS), amongst others. Some of you may have been on these courses.

I have long wanted to have a website as an extra resource for people who’ve been on my courses, and the 2020 lockdown – and my son Tim learning website design – have presented an ideal opportunity. I really enjoy teaching, and helping people gain skills with applications that I think are great. This website allows me to carry on doing that.

My background

I did a degree in Electronics, worked for IBM for 10 years in card and chip design and testing, moving into the technical side of marketing. I took a career break when I had my two children, during which time home computing boomed. I taught myself how to use a PC, then my mum, then various others. I took a part-time adult teaching qualification at Eastleigh College who then recruited me, and I have taught for them since 2007.

Back then there was generous funding from the EU to train people to use computers, particularly older people, and I taught internet, email, word processing, spreadsheets and even databases as part of ECDL and other qualifications. I started teaching digital photography and PaintShop Pro, moved into teaching BTEC and NCFE qualifications in photography and photo editing, and haven’t looked back.

Teaching

I take real pride in helping people learn, and in explaining concepts which can be quite hard to understand in the best possible way. I continually rethink my approach to try to improve, and I delight in people’s learning and increased capabilities and confidence. I see it as a way of empowering people, and I love to do that. I hope that my teaching offers a logical, calm and supportive way to learn. I am always willing to answer questions and provide extra help – please use the form on the contact page to get in touch.

If you would like more help, or to consult me about something in your setup, I offer one-to-one Photoshop, Elements, Lightroom and Photography teaching, currently over Zoom. My rate is £20 per half-hour. If you are interested, please get in touch using the button below.
