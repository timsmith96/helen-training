About

Hello and welcome to my website. My name is Helen and I teach photoshop and lightroom classes. For several years I have been teaching Photoshop and Lightroom courses in person - some of you may have been on these courses. I have set up this website in addition to my in-person classes as a means to post video tutorials on the topics I believe will be most useful to students.

You can see the available videos on the Photoshop or Lightroom pages, depending on what you're looking for. All of the videos can be accessed through the Videos page, where they are separated into various modules.
